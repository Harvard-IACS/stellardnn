About StellarDNN

The StellarDNN research lab, led by Pavlos Protopapas, explores machine learning algorithms and analysis tools to better understand and characterize astronomical and physical phenomena. From using neural networks to solve ODEs and PDEs, transformers to classify and characterize celestial objects from their brightness time series to euroencoders to capture the most essential features from galaxy images the group works on the intercection of physics and astronomy, machine learning and statistics.
